Our Story

Building the Bay Area with Pride

G De Leon Construction Inc. was founded with a simple mission: deliver quality construction with honest, transparent service. Based in San Rafael, we've built lasting relationships with homeowners throughout the Bay Area by treating every project like it's our own home.

As a licensed general contractor, plumber, and electrician, we bring a rare combination of skills under one roof. That means fewer subcontractors, better communication, and a smoother build process from start to finish. Whether you need a new deck, a full kitchen remodel, or a complete electrical upgrade, our team handles it all.
